Cursive Writing Tips

  • Start with proper posture: Sit up straight with feet flat on the floor
  • Hold your pen correctly: Use a tripod grip for better control
  • Practice letter connections: Focus on smooth transitions between letters
  • Maintain consistent slant: Keep all letters at the same angle
  • Use proper spacing: Leave appropriate gaps between words
  • Practice regularly: Daily practice leads to muscle memory development
